# ICB Site-Repo SEO Recommendations — 2026-06-15

**Source:** technical SEO audit (`seo/2026-06-15-icb-48p.md`), 48 pages, `--sample-per-type 5`.
**Audience:** Next.js site repo (icb-v2) dev. Every item here is a template / `generateMetadata()` / schema-component change — none are fixable in the AI-ops workspace.

> **Scope note:** SKU, roaster_profile, and roaster_lineup pages are programmatically generated (~1260 URLs total in sitemap). The audit sampled 5 of each — but because the findings are template-level, **a single fix per template propagates to every page of that type.** Affected-page counts below are sample counts; real reach is the full type.

## 🔴 High priority

### 1. `/learn` hub missing canonical + collection schema
`/learn` is a high-value hub page and is missing three signals at once:
- **Missing canonical tag** — add self-referencing `<link rel="canonical">`.
- **Missing `CollectionPage` JSON-LD.**
- **Missing `BreadcrumbList` JSON-LD.**

Fix in the `/learn` route's `generateMetadata()` + schema component.

### 2. Missing `BreadcrumbList` schema — 6 pages (template-level)
Affected: `/curations`, `/curations/coffee-lab`, `/learn`, `/roasters/partner`, `/roasters/terms`, `/tools`.
Collection templates (curations, tools, learn hub) and the secondary roaster_profile pages (`/roasters/partner`, `/roasters/terms`) emit no breadcrumb structured data. Add a shared `BreadcrumbList` JSON-LD helper to these layouts.

### 3. Missing `CollectionPage` schema — 4 pages (template-level)
Affected: `/curations`, `/curations/coffee-lab`, `/learn`, `/tools`.
The curations, tools, and learn-hub collection templates don't emit `CollectionPage`. (The main `/coffees` and `/roasters` collections do — use those as the reference implementation.)

### 4. `/contact` content too thin — 22 words
Risks "Crawled but not indexed." The contact template needs real indexable copy (what ICB is, how to reach the team, expected response, links to /about and /how-icb-works). Target ≥150 words.

### 4b. `/communities` content too thin — 23 words (collection min 50)
Surfaced after `/communities` was reclassified as a `collection` page (see item 11). Likely cause: the community cards/links render client-side and aren't counted as `<main>` body text. **Verify with a human eyeball** — if the listing is client-rendered, add an SSR'd intro paragraph describing what the communities directory is. As a `collection` page it also now inherits the `CollectionPage` + `BreadcrumbList` schema and `ItemList` recommendations below.

## 🟡 Medium priority

### 5. Title tags too long — 30 pages (template-level, `generateMetadata()`)
**Single highest-leverage fix.** Titles run 61–174 chars across nearly every type. Two root causes:
- **Doubled brand suffix.** Many titles append the brand twice, e.g. `/contact` → `Contact Us | Indian Coffee Beans Directory | Indian Coffee Beans`, and `/curations/coffee-lab` → `… | Indian Coffee Beans | Indian Coffee Beans`. Fix the base-title composer so the suffix is appended once.
- **Long SKU/roaster titles.** SKU titles concatenate full product names (e.g. the Coffee Nirvana blend at 174 chars). Truncate the dynamic portion so the composed title lands ≤60 chars.

Fixing the title composer once resolves the doubled-suffix sub-cluster fleet-wide.

### 6. Missing `ItemList` schema — 20 pages across collection-type templates (LLMO/GEO)
Helps AI crawlers (Google AI Overviews, etc.) enumerate the set. Add an `ItemList` (top 10–20 items) to:
- discovery (`/coffees/{brew}`) — 5 sampled
- learn_category (`/learn/category/*`) — 5 sampled
- roaster_lineup (`/roasters/*/coffees`) — 5 sampled
- collection (`/coffees`, `/curations`, `/learn`, `/roasters`) — 4
- curation_detail (`/curations/coffee-lab`) — 1
One shared `ItemList` helper wired into the collection-type layouts covers all of these.

### 7. Images missing alt text — 22 pages (template-level)
Consistently ~3 of ~7 images per page lack alt, plus 9/22 on the homepage. Pattern points to shared header/footer/UI/decorative images. Add alt text (or `alt=""` for purely decorative) in the shared components, and ensure dynamic images (logos, coffee photos) derive alt from their data.

### 8. Content depth nudges (template intros)
- `/curations/coffee-lab` — 199 words (target 250+). Curation_detail template could use a richer intro block.
- (Article-side depth items are routed to the content pipeline — see below.)

## ⚪ Low priority

### 9. Meta descriptions out of range — 27 pages (`generateMetadata()`)
14 too long (trim to ≤160), 13 too short (expand to ≥120). Spread across articles, roaster, collection, learn_category, and legal pages. Tune per-template description templates / fallbacks.

### 10. Heading hierarchy skips — 9 pages
H2→H4 on 7 pages (`/curations`, `/data-deletion`, `/privacy`, `/roasters/partner`, `/tools`, `/tools/coffee-calculator`, `/tools/expert-recipes`); H1→H4 on `/communities` and `/contact`. Use sequential levels (H1→H2→H3) — usually a styling-driven heading-level choice, not a structural need.

### 11. Page-type coverage gap (audit config) — ✅ RESOLVED 2026-06-16

All five SEO skills share this `page_types` block, so the weekly `seo-agent` and content skills can now route these pages too. Re-audit confirmed: `/privacy` and `/terms` clean, `/data-deletion` → item 12 below, `/communities` → item 4b above.
